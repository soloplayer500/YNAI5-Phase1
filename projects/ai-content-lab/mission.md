# AI Content Lab — Mission

## North Star
Build a content engine that produces 14+ short-form videos per week across TikTok / Reels /
Shorts, fully automated end-to-end (trend → script → voice → video → caption → upload),
with the operator only approving the final upload.

## Why It Exists
- Audience accumulation is the only sustainable distribution moat for the crypto-alpha lane.
- The infrastructure (skills, scripts, queue/) already exists — only the video-gen credit gate is missing.
- Long-term: ad revenue + brand deals + cross-promo to Block Syndicate VIP.

## Three Outcomes (ordered)
1. **First upload** within 7 days of unlock (Kling AI purchased)
2. **Daily cadence** within 21 days (1+ video/day, automated)
3. **First $100 ad/brand revenue** within 90 days of daily cadence

## Operating Principles (when active)
- Niche rotation: Mon/Fri = AI News, Tue/Thu/Sun = Brainrot, Wed/Sat = Fantasy
- Never upload without operator pre-roll review (1-min per video max)
- Voice-over via ElevenLabs free tier (10K chars/mo) until revenue proves model
- B-roll free tier (Pexels) before paying for stock

## Non-Negotiables
- No YouTube monetization spam — every video must clear policy review
- No copyrighted music — Suno-generated audio only
- No fake claims, no sensational thumbnails, no rage-bait
- Brand consistency: YNAI5 logo + character bible enforced in every frame
