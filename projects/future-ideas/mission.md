# Future Ideas — Mission

## North Star
Be the operator's **safe parking lot** for concepts that aren't ready for a revenue lane,
without destroying optionality by deleting them.

## Why This Lane Exists
Solo builders accumulate concepts faster than they can validate them. Without a parking lot,
two failure modes happen:

1. **Concept-creep:** new ideas leak into active lanes → divides focus → revenue stalls
2. **Premature deletion:** ideas get killed before their moment → operator loses optionality

Future-ideas is the explicit space where ideas wait without polluting active execution.

## Three Outcomes (ordered)
1. **Zero leakage:** no future-ideas concept gets executed without explicit promotion
2. **Quarterly review:** every entry reviewed once per quarter (kill / promote / re-park)
3. **High-quality promotions:** at most 1 promotion per quarter to active lane

## Operating Principles
- **Promotion is rare.** Default answer to "should I promote?" is NO.
- **Killing is also rare.** Default answer to "should I delete?" is NO. Park, don't delete.
- **No work happens here.** No code runs, no scripts execute, no agents activate.
- **Annual cleanup, not monthly.** The point is to be a low-pressure shelf.

## Non-Negotiables
- No future-idea is allowed to consume operator time during active-lane work hours
- No promotion without revenue model + ≤$50 / ≤8 hour test plan + kill criterion
- No deletion without 30-day notice in `logs.md`
- The folder is read-only during a working session UNLESS quarterly review is happening
