# Crypto Alpha — Mission

## North Star
Generate sovereign monthly recurring revenue from crypto/stock signal intelligence,
while compounding the operator's own portfolio via a tracked, transparent edge.

## Why It Exists
1. The screener already runs. Daily output already exists. The only missing piece was distribution + payment.
2. Kraken portfolio is underwater — recovering it requires a system, not vibes.
3. Trading edge is built by logging predictions and scoring them weekly. No logging = no edge.

## Three Outcomes (ordered)
1. **VIP MRR** — first paid subscriber within 14 days, 10 within 30 days, 25–80 by month 3
2. **Edge data** — 70%+ accuracy across 20 logged predictions before scaling position sizes
3. **Capital recovery** — Kraken positions return to ≥ break-even by Q3

## Operating Principles
- Same filter for every pick. No discretion = no excuses.
- Win rate is a vanity metric. Expectancy is the real one.
- Free channel publishes EVERYTHING that VIP gets a stripped version of.
- Public log of wins AND losses. Transparency > hype.
- Never recommend a position the operator wouldn't take.

## Non-Negotiables
- Never publish a signal that hasn't been generated by the screener (no manual "alpha drops")
- Never raise VIP price below 30 paying subscribers
- Never add a new asset to the screener until 30 days of clean data exists for the current set
