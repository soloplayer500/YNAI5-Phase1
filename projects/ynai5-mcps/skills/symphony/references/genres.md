# SYMPHONY — Genre Profiles

## Trap

**Sonic signature:** Hard 808s, hi-hat rolls, punchy snares, dark/minor key melodies
**Tempo range:** 130-160 BPM (trap), 70-85 BPM (half-time feel in writing)
**Flow patterns:** Triplet flow, double-time, syncopated delivery
**Common themes:** Money, loyalty, street life, flexing, paranoia, come-up

**Suno style tags:** `trap, hard trap, 808s, dark trap, melodic trap, drill`
**Instrument tags:** `808 bass, trap hi-hats, snare, piano, strings, dark synth`

**Lyric characteristics:**
- Short punchy bars (4-8 syllables per line common)
- Heavy ad-lib notation: `[ad libs: skrrt, yeah, gang]`
- Internal rhyme schemes more than end rhyme
- Repetition in hooks for hypnotic effect

---

## Melodic Rap / Emo Rap

**Sonic signature:** Atmospheric beats, heavy 808s but melodic, vocal-forward production
**Tempo range:** 60-100 BPM, slower feel
**Flow patterns:** Sung-rap hybrid, melismatic delivery, pitch variation noted
**Common themes:** Pain, loss, love, loyalty, anxiety, flex through pain

**Suno style tags:** `melodic rap, emo rap, sad trap, atmospheric, lo-fi trap`
**Instrument tags:** `guitar, piano, strings, 808, reverb, atmospheric pads`

**Lyric characteristics:**
- Longer melodic lines that stretch across bars
- Phonetic spelling for Suno: "ba-a-aby", "sta-a-ay"
- Emotional vulnerability mixed with street flex
- Bridge sections are critical — emotional peak

---

## R&B / Trap R&B

**Sonic signature:** Smooth production, live instruments or sampled soul, 808s lighter
**Tempo range:** 60-90 BPM
**Flow patterns:** Sung, falsetto moments, melodic rap sections
**Common themes:** Love, relationships, late nights, vibes, loyalty

**Suno style tags:** `R&B, trap R&B, contemporary R&B, smooth, neo soul`
**Instrument tags:** `piano, guitar, bass, drums, strings, saxophone`

**Lyric characteristics:**
- More complete sentences than trap
- Vocal runs noted: "[vocal run on 'love']"
- Pre-chorus builds are important
- Harmonies flagged: "[harmony: stack on chorus]"

---

## Drill

**Sonic signature:** Dark, minimal, sliding 808s, staccato hi-hats
**Tempo range:** 140-150 BPM
**Flow patterns:** Monotone delivery, aggressive, matter-of-fact
**Common themes:** Street, threat, survival, neighborhood, real talk

**Suno style tags:** `drill, UK drill, Chicago drill, dark, aggressive`
**Instrument tags:** `sliding 808, dark piano, snare, minimal hi-hat`

---

## Suno Section Tag Reference

```
[intro]           - Opening 4-8 bars
[verse]           - Main rap verse (16 bars standard)
[rap verse]       - Signals harder rap delivery to Suno
[melodic verse]   - Signals sung/melodic delivery
[pre-chorus]      - Build before chorus
[chorus]          - Main hook (repeat-ready)
[hook]            - Shorter hook variant
[bridge]          - Contrasting mid-song section
[outro]           - Closing bars
[instrumental]    - Marks a section with no vocals
[ad libs: ...]    - Specifies ad lib words for Suno
[harmony: ...]    - Requests vocal layering
[breakdown]       - Energy drop section
[build]           - Energy rising section
```
