# SYMPHONY — Artist DNA Map

## NBA YoungBoy

**Flow:** Aggressive, emotional, fast-switching between melodic and hard rap. Often starts bars slow then accelerates.
**Cadence:** Punchy, clipped syllables. Heavy on triplets. Unpredictable rhythm changes.
**Voice tags for Suno:** `energetic male rapper, raspy, aggressive, Louisiana rap`
**Vocabulary:** "gang", "on God", "slime", "real", "loyalty", "keep it real", "since a youngin"
**Themes:** Loyalty above everything, street survival, emotional pain masked by aggression, trust issues, come-up
**Signature moves:** Starts hook repetitive and hypnotic. Switches from rage to vulnerability mid-song.
**Suno style tags:** `NBA YoungBoy style, Baton Rouge rap, aggressive melodic trap, emotional trap`

---

## Gunna

**Flow:** Smooth, drawled, slow drip delivery. Luxury trap.
**Cadence:** Slow, deliberate, every word lands softly. Melodic throughout.
**Voice tags for Suno:** `smooth male rapper, melodic, Atlanta trap, luxury`
**Vocabulary:** "drip", "sauce", "wunna", "pushin P", designer brands, lifestyle flex
**Themes:** Luxury lifestyle, fashion, women, flex, loyalty to the plug
**Signature moves:** Hook is simple and sticky. Verses don't rush — let beats breathe.
**Suno style tags:** `Gunna style, drip season, Atlanta melodic trap, luxury trap`

---

## Juice WRLD

**Flow:** Freestyle-feel, melodic rap that blurs line between rap and singing.
**Cadence:** Fluid, no hard stops. Lines connect seamlessly.
**Voice tags for Suno:** `melodic rapper, emo rap, singing rapper, Chicago`
**Vocabulary:** "Legends Never Die", "Lucy", pain references, drug references (use carefully), heartbreak
**Themes:** Heartbreak, anxiety, drug use (handle with care), fame pressure, loyalty, living fast
**Signature moves:** Chorus is sung, verse slides between rap and melody. Emotional peak in bridge.
**Suno style tags:** `Juice WRLD style, emo rap, melodic rap, sad trap, Chicago`

---

## Rod Wave

**Flow:** Deeply melodic, soul-influenced. More singing than rapping.
**Cadence:** Slow, gospel-influenced. Long held notes.
**Voice tags for Suno:** `soulful male singer, emotional, melodic, Florida`
**Vocabulary:** "God", family, struggle, perseverance, real talk, come from nothing
**Themes:** Faith, struggle, growth, coming from nothing, street redemption
**Signature moves:** Bridge is the emotional climax. Hooks are anthemic and singable.
**Suno style tags:** `Rod Wave style, soul trap, melodic R&B rap, emotional, Florida rap`

---

## Lil Baby

**Flow:** Fast, locked-in triplet. Very rhythmic and locked to the beat.
**Cadence:** Staccato, machine-gun delivery. Consistent tempo throughout.
**Voice tags for Suno:** `Atlanta rapper, fast flow, street rap, energetic`
**Vocabulary:** "4PF", "drip", real/fake divide, loyalty, money moves, come-up
**Themes:** Loyalty, money, coming up from nothing, real vs fake, street credibility
**Signature moves:** Never lets up pace. Hook is usually just 2-4 bars on repeat. Verses are dense.
**Suno style tags:** `Lil Baby style, Atlanta trap, fast flow, street rap`

---

## Drake

**Flow:** Versatile — switches between singing and rapping seamlessly.
**Cadence:** Adaptable to beat. Can be slow/melodic or rapid/aggressive.
**Voice tags for Suno:** `Toronto rapper, melodic, versatile, R&B rap`
**Vocabulary:** "OVO", relationships, city, success, exes, loyalty doubts
**Themes:** Relationships, success, haters, loyalty, Toronto pride, fame and its costs
**Signature moves:** Pre-chorus builds tension. Hooks are incredibly catchy and R&B-adjacent.
**Suno style tags:** `Drake style, Toronto rap, OVO, melodic rap, R&B trap`

## YNAI5 Voice (Solo's Original Voice)

**Core identity:** Young, Aruban, 25, loyalty-first, AI-forward, ambitious solo operator
**Flow:** Confident, measured. Can go hard when needed but never forced.
**Cadence:** Caribbean-influenced rhythm, Gen-Z natural speech patterns
**Vocabulary:** Authentic to Solo — no forced slang. Loyalty, AI, grind, YNAI5, real ones
**Themes:** Building something real, loyalty above clout, AI as power tool, solo grind, Aruba pride
**Voice blend rule:** Solo's voice = 60% minimum. Artist DNA = max 40%. Never lose the authentic voice.
**Suno voice tags:** `young male rapper, confident, Caribbean influence, melodic trap`
