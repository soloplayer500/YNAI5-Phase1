The 2% rule.

Never risk more than 2% of your total portfolio on a single trade.

If your portfolio is $1,000 → max loss per trade: $20

Sounds small. It keeps you in the game.

**Tweet 3:**
Here's how to apply it:

Entry: $100
Stop: $92 (8% below entry)
Portfolio: $1,000

Position size = (2% × $1,000) ÷ 8% = $250

You buy $250 worth. If it hits stop, you lose $20. Portfolio survives.

**Tweet 4:**
