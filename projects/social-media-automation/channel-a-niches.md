## Niche 1 — AI Brainrot
**Format**: Chaotic 15–30s clips, fast cuts, absurd AI-generated visuals + meme audio
**Why**: Highest virality ceiling. TikTok algo loves chaos + watch loops.
**Content types**:
- AI generates increasingly unhinged versions of everyday things
- "What if [mundane thing] but AI turned it into chaos"
- AI voice acting over viral meme scripts
- Unexpected AI image/video outputs reacted to

**Prompt style for Kling**: high energy, surreal motion, exaggerated colors, chaotic transitions
**Audio**: trending TikTok sounds (check /trend-check) or ElevenLabs VO for narration
**Posting cadence**: 1/day TikTok, mirror to Shorts

**Example titles**:
- "I asked AI to make breakfast more extreme"
- "AI trying to understand human emotions be like"
- "Day 47 of making AI recreate [viral thing]"

---

## Niche 2 — Elemental / Fantasy AI
**Format**: 20–45s showcase clips — "AI generates X armor/weapon/world"
**Why**: High saves + shares. Aspirational content = strong retention signal.
**Content types**:
- Elemental armor series: Fire, Ice, Shadow, Void, Storm — one per video
- "AI builds [fantasy creature] in [style]" e.g. Dark Souls, Anime, Minecraft
- AI-generated world-building: environments, kingdoms, lore drops
- "AI redraws [iconic game/anime character] in [style]"

**Prompt style for Kling**: cinematic reveal, dramatic lighting, fantasy aesthetic, slow zoom
**Audio**: epic/orchestral BGM or trending fantasy sounds
**Posting cadence**: 3–4x/week (slightly more production thought per video)

**Example titles**:
- "AI designed the VOID armor and it's terrifying"
- "What every element looks like as armor — AI edition"
- "AI rebuilt the Dark Souls world in anime style"

---

## Niche 3 — AI News Reactions
**Format**: 30–60s commentary clips — Claude researches, scripts, voice-over plays over visuals
**Why**: Evergreen + searchable. Builds authority. Easy to batch.
**Content types**:
- "X just dropped [new AI model] — here's what it actually means"
- "AI did [thing] today and nobody's talking about it"
- Breaking AI news simplified in 30 seconds
- "Claude vs GPT vs Gemini — who actually won [week]"

**Prompt style for Kling**: clean tech aesthetic, talking head style OR news-ticker overlay
**Audio**: ElevenLabs VO (Claude writes script → /voice-gen)
**Posting cadence**: whenever major AI news drops (2–3x/week min)

**Example titles**:
- "GPT-5 just leaked and it's wild"
- "This AI news changes everything for creators"
- "The AI nobody's talking about this week"
