## CLIP 1 — The Arrival (eerie, simple)

```
2D anime cartoon style, flat cell-shaded colors, no photorealism.

The protagonist appears from darkness — he steps forward slowly out of black fog,
purple glowing eyes visible first before the rest of his body. Red and green skulls
float in slowly from both sides of the frame, orbiting him loosely.

He stops center frame. Looks directly into camera. Eyes pulse once — bright flash
of purple. Skulls freeze in place.

Background: pure black. Character is the only subject.
Camera: completely static. No movement. Let him fill the frame.

Style: flat 2D anime, bold outlines, minimal shading, neon glow on eyes and skulls.
```

## CLIP 2 — Power Moment (simple, impactful)

```
2D anime cartoon style, flat cell-shaded illustration aesthetic.

The protagonist stands centered, arms slightly out, chin slightly down — power stance.
Behind him: a slow circular ring of floating skulls (red, green, purple) rotating
clockwise at a steady pace. Each skull glows with a different color.

His eyes open from closed — slow reveal. Purple glow fills the screen from his eyes
outward like a light bloom. The skull ring speeds up as the glow intensifies.

At second 7 — everything pulses outward in one shockwave. Skulls scatter to edges of
frame. Glow fades. He returns to still.

Background: deep dark purple gradient. No environment. Abstract.
Camera: completely static.

Style: 2D anime flat, bold cell outlines, neon palette — purple, red, green on black.
```

**VO (ElevenLabs, layer in CapCut):**
> *"This is just the beginning."*

## CLIP 3 — The Stare (shortest, simplest — strongest hook)

```
2D anime cartoon style, flat illustration.

Extreme close-up on the protagonist's face — from nose to top of head, 9:16.
Eyes closed. Completely still.

One red skull drifts into frame from the left. Stops beside his head. Waits.
One green skull drifts in from the right. Stops. Waits.

Silence. 4 full seconds of stillness.

Then — his eyes open. Slowly. Purple glow builds from nothing to blinding.
The skulls react — they vibrate, jitter, then vanish instantly.

His expression: does not change.
Cut to black.

Style: 2D anime flat, extreme close-up, maximum impact from stillness and contrast.
No camera movement. No environment. Just the face.
```

**VO (ElevenLabs, layer in CapCut):**
> *(no VO on this one — pure silence makes it hit harder)*
> *(optional: add a single low bass note in CapCut at eye-open moment)*

## Why Cartoon Works Better Right Now
- Simpler prompts = fewer Sora errors / artifacts
- Our ref images ARE already cartoon — Sora matches the style naturally
- No physics to get wrong (no rain, no destruction, no fire physics)
- Flat 2D anime looks intentional — not "AI tried to be realistic and failed"
- Faster iteration — generate, check, adjust prompt, regenerate

## Production Stack for This Batch
1. Upload `ref-08` to Sora as reference
2. Paste prompt, 9:16, 8 sec
3. Generate → download
4. CapCut: add ElevenLabs VO (only on clips 1 and 2)
5. Add Suno dark ambient track underneath (low volume)
6. Auto-captions in CapCut
7. Upload as OpenMindAI

## Start with Clip 3 (The Stare)
— Simplest prompt, most static, lowest chance of Sora artifacts
— No VO needed = even faster to produce
— Stillness + sudden eye-open = natural rewatch mechanic
