# Decision: TikTok Niche MVP Strategy
_Date: 2026-03-09_

## Context
Had full niche analysis done (AI Tools + Finance Hybrid, 9/10 score). User chose to park that and start with a faster, lower-friction entry point instead.

## Decision
**Start TikTok with "AI News but funny/relatable" format.**
- Capture the normal-user TikTok energy — rapid, shareable, noticeable
- Light AI/tech news angle — "did you see what [Claude/Gemini] just did??"
- Cliché funny reactions to real AI updates and moments
- Goal: build audience + collect data on what works, THEN go deeper into the higher-RPM niche

## Reasoning
- Fastest path to first upload — no deep research needed, news is always happening
- Algorithm rewards authentic, casual content over polished AI slop
- Build an audience FIRST, then monetize — don't optimize for RPM before you have viewers
- Low production cost — can batch 5 videos in a night
- Teaches us what resonates before committing to a full production pipeline

## Format Defined
- Hook: "bro did you see what [AI] just did??" / "nobody's talking about this"
- Body: 15–30 sec, show the actual thing, react to it
- End: slight cliffhanger or punchline + "follow for AI updates"
- Tone: casual, funny, slightly shocked — like texting a friend

## What We're NOT Doing Yet
- Deep finance/crypto explainers (saved in niche-analysis-2026.md)
- YNAI5 World cartoon universe (saved, long game)
- Full production pipeline (Sora + CapCut + voice) — comes after first traction
