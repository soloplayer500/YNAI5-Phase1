## Details by Theme

### Theme 1: What's Proven to Make Money
- **Reddit Story Compilations**: Simplest format. One channel documented at $46K/month. $11 RPM. Top video: 2.6M views, ~$30K from one video.
- **True Crime**: Long-form (15–30 min). 70–82% retention. Estimated $80K+/month at 2M subscribers.
- **AI News / Tools**: Channels that explain new AI tools (Claude, ChatGPT, Midjourney updates) perform consistently. Demystifying AI = high demand.

### Theme 2: What's Growing Fast Right Now
- **TikTok AI Storytelling**: Full stack automation possible. ChatGPT writes script → AI voice → AI video. Creator Fund pays up to $25K on viral videos.
- **ChatGPT Workflow Tutorials**: Show people HOW to use AI. 120% YoY growth on TikTok. High intent audience.
- **Animated Object Monologues**: New format exploding on TikTok. Inanimate object (a chair, a coin, etc.) delivers philosophical monologue. ChatGPT writes it, VEO 3 generates it.

### Theme 3: Retention Architecture (Critical)
- YouTube Shorts "explore and exploit" model: 75% retention with 10K views > 40% retention with 100K views
- Sweet spot for long-form ad revenue: 8+ minutes (enables mid-roll ads)
- Hooks in first 2 seconds on TikTok are non-negotiable
- Pattern interrupts every 15–20 seconds to maintain watch time

## YNAI5 World — Niche Recommendations

Given Shami's assets (Suno, Claude, crypto knowledge, AI infrastructure):

### 🥇 Best Fit: AI Tools + Automation Content
- **Why**: High CPM ($10–15), massive growth, matches existing expertise
- **Format**: "How I use [AI tool] to do [thing]" — tutorials, workflows, breakdowns
- **TikTok**: Quick 30–60s demos. YouTube: 8–15 min deep dives.

### 🥈 Strong Secondary: Crypto + AI Commentary
- **Why**: Combines two areas of expertise. Crypto news + AI analysis angle = unique
- **Format**: "What AI says about [crypto event]" — AI-generated commentary on market moves
- **Audience**: Crypto traders who also follow AI

### 🥉 Long Game: AI Music (Suno) + Story Visuals
- **Why**: Suno is a real differentiator. Most channels don't have original music.
- **Format**: Short AI music clips with AI visuals on TikTok → full tracks on YouTube
- **Blocker**: Needs Distrokid for distribution. TikTok can start now without it.

### ❌ Avoid Right Now
- Pure AI image slideshows (getting flagged/removed)
- Template-based motivational quote videos (oversaturated + crackdown risk)
- Voiceover-only Reddit reposts with no original angle (dying format)
