# Tool Review: InVideo AI
Date: 2026-03-09
URL: https://ai.invideo.io
Tested by: Shami (Solo)
Verdict: ❌ NOT suitable for YNAI World content

---

## What InVideo AI Actually Is
InVideo is NOT a generative AI video tool like Sora or Veo.
It is a **stock media assembler** — it takes a script or prompt and stitches together:
- Stock footage clips
- Pre-designed templates
- AI-generated voiceover
- Text overlays

It cannot generate original characters, original worlds, or AI-visualized content.
That is why the YNAI protagonist did not appear — InVideo has no idea who he is.

## Test Results (Shami's Test — 2026-03-09)
- Output length: ~5 seconds (free tier limitation)
- YNAI character: did not appear (stock footage only)
- Quality rating: low — generic stock feel, no original generation
- Verdict: wrong tool entirely for YNAI World

## What It's Actually Good For
- Marketing videos for businesses
- High-volume content with stock footage
- Voiceover + script assembly
- Teams that need fast "professional-looking" (not original) output

## Why It Seemed Relevant
InVideo became OpenAI's first official Sora 2 partner (Oct 2025) and also integrated Veo 3.1.
So it CAN use Sora/Veo under the hood — but as a template-first platform, not raw generation.
Future use: could be useful for templated content at scale AFTER we have a defined format.

## Decision
**Parked.** Revisit if we ever need high-volume template-based content.
For YNAI World: use Sora directly (ChatGPT Pro) or Veo 3.1 (AI Studio).
